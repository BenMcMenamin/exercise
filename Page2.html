Universal Design

Your webpage incorporates Universal Design principles in the following ways:

  • Ensuring accessibility: The webpage follows accessibility guidelines, such as providing proper labels, meaningful error messages, and keyboard navigation.
  • Clear instructions: Instructions and examples are provided near input fields to guide users in providing the correct information.
  • Contrasting colors and readable fonts: The webpage uses colors with sufficient contrast for improved readability, and easy-to-read fonts.
  • Alternative text for images: All images used in the webpage have descriptive alt text for screen readers.
  • Testing with assistive technologies: The webpage has been tested with screen readers and other assistive technologies to ensure its accessibility and usability for a wide range of users.
